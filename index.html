How It Works

This ia an application that enables you get your friends,family or colleague together to contribute towards individual goals

The contibution is done on a monthly basis in which one member of the group takes the lump sum contributed. A member creates a group,assigns a name to the group,sets amount to be contributed and sends out an invite linke automatically to other members to join the created group , This member is assigned Group admin status. Each member joins their created group via the link sent out by the group admin and is assigned a number on joining the group, this number determines members turn of accessing the fund generated.

The said contribution is a fixed sum decided by the group admin. This amount is deducted at a particular day every month , which is also decided upon by the groups admin and payment is made to the member whoose turn it is.
